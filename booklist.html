Book Recommendations


My Interest







Adult Books

There is nothing like a good book. Below are some books that I really enjoyed reading, and would recommend them to you:


  • The Nightingale by Kristin Hannah
  • The Lilac Girls by Martha Hall Kelly
  • Before We Were Yours by Lisa Wingate





Many, if not all, of these books can be purchased directly from Amazon.

Childrens Books

Below are some childrens books that I recommend. I have read many of these to my own children and my students.


  • The Legend of Rock Paper Scissors by Drew Daywalt
  • Caps for Sale by Esphyr Slobodkina
  • The Miraculous Journey of Edward Tulane by Kate DiCamillo
  • The Chocolate Touch by Patrick Skene Catling
  • Tops & Bottoms by Janet Stevens
  • Can I Play Too by Mo Willems
  • Sylvester and the Magic Pebble by William Steig
  • Dog vs. Cat by Chris Gall

Many, if not all, of these books can be purchased directly from Amazon.
